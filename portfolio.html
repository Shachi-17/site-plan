About Me

Hi I'm Sharlene Aniel Dela Cruz, 19 years old from Philippines. I opted for a domain name that reflected my identity as a photographer, something simple yet memorable. The choice of a hosting provider was equally important, as it would determine the website's performance and accessibility. I selected a reliable hosting service that ensured my website would load quickly and remain accessible at all times. Once the technical aspects were sorted, it was time to design the website. I wanted a clean and visually appealing layout that would highlight my photography and provide an excellent user experience. After researching website design options, I decided to use a content management system (CMS) that allowed me to create a visually stunning and easy-to-navigate site without the need for extensive coding skills. The website's homepage features a captivating image slider that showcases some of my best work. Each image is carefully chosen to represent different styles and themes, giving visitors a taste of my diverse photography portfolio. A well-crafted introduction on the homepage provides a brief overview of my photography journey, emphasizing my passion and dedication to the art. The portfolio section of the website is the heart of my online presence. It's where visitors can explore my collection of photographs, organized into categories such as landscapes, portraits, and street photography. Each image is accompanied by a brief description that provides context, tells a story, or shares technical details about the shot. The portfolio is a testament to the diversity of my work and my ability to capture beauty in various settings and subjects. One of the unique features of my website is the "Behind the Lens" section. In this blog-style section, I share the stories behind some of my most memorable shots. It's an opportunity to give visitors a glimpse into the creative process, the challenges I faced, and the emotions that inspired each photograph. By sharing these stories, I hope to foster a deeper connection with my audience and showcase the passion that drives my work. In addition to the portfolio and "Behind the Lens" sections, I regularly update the website with informative blog posts. These posts cover a wide range of topics related to photography, including tips for beginners, equipment reviews, and discussions on photography as an art form. My aim is to provide valuable content that helps aspiring photographers improve their skills and gain a deeper appreciation for the art of photography. A contact page is also an essential component of the website.
